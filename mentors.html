Dr. Said Bettayeb, Ph.D

Dr. Said Bettayeb received his Ph.D. in Computer Science from Northwestern University. He has supervised 7 Ph.D. dissertations, over 100 M.S. Thesis, and B.S. Honor Thesis/Projects. Dr. Bettayeb has served on the editorial boards of several journals and various conference program committees, steering committees, and advisory committees. His research focuses on the areas of Algorithm Design, Parallel and Distributed Computing, VLSI Design and Computation, Grid Computing, Information Security, and Bioinformatics. Dr. Bettayeb is a senior member of IEEE.

Iqbal Latheef
